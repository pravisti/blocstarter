What is BlocStarter?


BlocStarter is the first place to turn when you or someone you love is facing a challenge. It’s the central rallying place for giving and receiving meaningful support. From sending a simple “Thinking of You” to raising money for out-of-pocket expenses, BlocStarter empowers anyone tobuild a community and take action when it counts.
